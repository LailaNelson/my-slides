What are Text Features?

Text features are all the components of a story or article that are not the main body of text. They help your brain organize information, preview concepts, and navigate complex text smoothly!

4th Grade ELA: Text Features Explorers

The Explorer Toolkit

Think of text features as structural signposts. Just like a map needs legends, scales, and labels, an informational text needs titles, headings, sidebars, and captions to guide its audience.
